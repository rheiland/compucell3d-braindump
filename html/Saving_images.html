Saving images in CC3D

In an email discussion that started with Yi (at LANL), asking about the functionality of the 'camera' icon in the CC3D GUI, I learned some things. Further discussions with Mitja and Julio led to further understanding and the discovery of bugs in the functionality.

For starters, the function of the 'camera' icon in the toolbar of a Graphics Window does *not* mean to simply take a snapshot of what's in the graphics window. What it does do is "remember" the current field and orientation that is displayed and dump images using that info.
