Workflow paper

From CompuCell3D Wiki

Jump to: navigation, search

We had our paper, "Workflows for Parameter Studies of Multi-Cell Modeling", accepted to this conf http://hosting.cs.vt.edu/hpc2010/ in April. This was basically demonstrating a use case of VisTrails and cc3D being used for parameter sweeps (for cell sorting) and doing the runs on Big Red.
